## FLYWHEEL ACCELERATION TACTICS

### How to Make the Flywheel Spin Faster

#### 1. Reduce Friction at Each Stage

**Problem:** Long sales cycles (6-9 months average)
**Solution:**
- Free executive workshop (demonstrate value before asking for commitment)
- Pilot programs ($10K for 1 department, 30 days)
- Video testimonials (address objections preemptively)
- ROI calculator (quantify value instantly)

**Impact:** Sales cycle reduced to 3-4 months

---

#### 2. Increase Velocity at Each Stage

**Problem:** Too much time between stages
**Solution:**
- Automate lead nurture (email sequences, content journeys)
- Next-step clarity (every meeting ends with scheduled follow-up)
- Decision-making frameworks (help buyers build internal consensus faster)
- Executive alignment calls (get all stakeholders on same call)

**Impact:** 30% reduction in time-to-close

---

#### 3. Amplify Success Stories

**Problem:** Great results but not enough people know
**Solution:**
- Customer marketing manager role (dedicated to storytelling)
- Video case study library (easier to consume than written)
- Customer conference (bring advocates together annually)
- Co-marketing programs (joint webinars, co-authored content)
- Award submissions (external validation)

**Impact:** 3x increase in referral-sourced pipeline

---

#### 4. Systematize Expansion

**Problem:** Account managers reactive, not proactive on upsells
**Solution:**
- Expansion playbook (triggers, timing, messaging for each path)
- Quarterly business reviews (uncover new needs proactively)
- Usage analytics (identify departments ready for expansion)
- Success milestones (celebrate wins, then suggest next step)

**Impact:** NRR increases from 110% to 130%

---

#### 5. Create Compounding Effects

**Problem:** Each sale treated as one-off transaction
**Solution:**
- Customer advisory board (10-15 champions shape product)
- Annual user conference (community building + upsell opportunities)
- Certification program (customers become authorized trainers)
- Partner ecosystem (customers introduce complementary vendors)

**Impact:** Ecosystem creates defensible moat, sticky customers
